About

I graduated college from Brigham Young University with a bachelors degree in Exercise Science. During my time at BYU I also played on the rugby team winning 3 national championships and second team all american honors. After graduating from BYU then I worked at a software company named IDS. That is where I was introduced to coding and realized that is the career I wanted to be. I started to learn to code on my own and after a few months decided to enroll in a program to ensure that I had a strong knowledge base to start with. I chose Lambda school since they had a computer science section to go along with learning web development. After finishing up at Lambda school then I started to do some freelance work for a company name Create Room and shortly after started working as a full stack web developer for NewAge.

At NewAge my first project was our convergence project where we converged all partner company websites and systems into one. I mostly focused on the frontend and built the product page, shop page, put together the homepage, many sections of our enrollment process, the many modals in the site, and much more. I also helped customize the site for different global markets and helped create pages and sections in our CMS. Since the project has launched then I have helped maintain the frontend and backend code, fixed bugs, and implemented new market specific features in both the frontend and backend aspects of the site. I have also started on other web application projects that utilize different tech stacks have taken the lead on one of those projects.

I currently live in Utah with my wife, Corina, and our three kids, Fox(4), Grey(2), and Magnolia (newborn).
